About iLibrary

iLibrary is an initiative which was started by Raphael Limbikani Mbewe, in 2018 to help people how can not afford to buy books gain access to books across all the 10 provinces of Zambia. iLibrary operates by recieving books from different individuals who have finished using them and are willing to pass them to the next person.Most school books are given to community schools. The main goal of this initiative to increase literacy levels among Zambians. Who is a donner? any person willing to donate a book.
